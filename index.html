Important Information

Marathon Format:

Marathons will take place in person 9:00-14:30.

The Marathons will be instructed by student tutors from the RRIS.

The marathon is a 120 NIS for a 5-hour, one-time session to review the relevant material of the course.

Please note, the tutor will not be reviewing all of the material taught in the course.

Registration & Refunds:

The marathon will be confirmed and take place only if a minimum of 5 paying students register at least 48 hours in advance.

Tickets are non-refundable!

The only option for a refund is if the marathon is canceled.

For Reservists:

Reservists in groups 2 & 3 attend for free, but must register - confirmation of reserve duty will be checked in accordance with our records.

For any questions on this matter, please reach out to Lisa Sharon - lisa.sharon@runi.ac.il.

Location:

Location details for the Marathon will be provided by the tutor shortly before the Marathon. (Marathons can be either on campus or on zoom)

The marathons are for courses taught in the RRIS.
