Important Information

For RRIS Students Only:

Dear Students, these marathons are meant for RRIS students only! and will be conducted in ENGLISH

We need 7 students to register and pay in order to open the marathon. Once the marathon is confirmed, the tickets are nonrefundable. If we don't have 7 students registered for the marathon, the marathon will be cancelled and you will receive a refund.

Reservists attend for free based on which Miluim group you are in. Please contact Lisa Sharon - Lisa.sharon@runi.ac.il.

Marathon Format:

The zoom link for the Marathon will be provided by the tutor in a confirmation email a few days before the Marathon. Marathons will take place on zoom and some have the option for hybrid.
